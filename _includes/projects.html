University Major Project: Hnefatafl

  • Unity
  • .Net Multiplayer Server

My final year project for University was based on the digital recreation of an ancient medieval board game Hnefatafl, this is a variant of the wide spread game Tafl played mainly in Scandinavia. From the project I produced a working mobile application (Android) that could play two player hot-seat style games and online match making multiplayer.

The project allowed me to implement multiplayer techniques I had been experimenting with in personal time in a more professional capacity.
